Vanguard Shield

Vanguard Shield is a modern security simulation and system analysis tool designed to monitor device health and visualize network protocols.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data within the Vanguard Shield mobile application.

  • Data Collection: We collect Advertising ID (AD_ID) and basic device performance data (crash logs, diagnostics) via Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics.
  • Advertisements: Our app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These services may use the Advertising ID to show relevant ads to users.
  • Data Security: All data transmitted by the app is encrypted in transit using HTTPS protocols.
  • Family Policy: We comply with the Google Play Families Policy, ensuring that data collection for younger audiences is handled with extra care and in accordance with legal requirements.

Data Deletion

Since we do not require user accounts, we do not store personal identifiable information on our own servers. However, you can reset your Advertising ID via your Android device settings at any time.
